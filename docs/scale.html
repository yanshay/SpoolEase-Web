SpoolEase Scale

Take control of your filament usage with precision weight tracking. The SpoolEase Scale provides accurate measurement of net filament weight, helping you avoid running out of material during crucial prints.

Integrates seamlessly with your SpoolEase Console for a complete filament management system that optimizes your 3D printing workflow.

Install Firmware Build Information

Key Features

  • Precise Weight Measurement - Accurately measure the weight of your filament spools to track usage and remaining material.
  • Flexible Spool Core Configuration - Various options to configure weight of spool cores, ensuring accurate net filament weight calculation regardless of spool manufacturer.
  • Net Weight Display - See exactly how much filament is left without manual calculations, displayed clearly on the device.
  • Filament Tracking Foundation - The first critical step towards building a reliable filament tracking system for your entire printing setup.
  • Optional NFC Integration - Additional NFC scanning point available with focus on weight tracking use cases.

Important Notes

• For best results, calibrate the scale upon initial setup using a known weight reference.

• The scale is designed to work optimally with standard filament spool sizes. Oversized spools may require special configuration.

• Pair with the SpoolEase Console for a complete filament management ecosystem.
